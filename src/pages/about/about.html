Health, Safety and Wellbeing
Policy Summary

If you have raised a Water Quality or Environmental Hazard, please note that these events will not be accessible in the SafetyNet Desktop.

The Water Quality and Environment hazards are being managed off line by the respective teams.

Our vision for health, safety and wellbeing is that no-one gets hurt or made unwell by what we do.

This means caring for each other, our contractors and anyone who might come into contact with our activities.

Doing things the right way not only ensures that people don’t get hurt, it’s also more efficient and ensures that we deliver the service our customers deserve.

We need to build on our successes so far and continue to improve towards achieving zero incidents.

To do this we need to:

  • Recognise success
  • Lead by example
  • Deliver safe environments, systems and behaviours
  • Develop and follow practical standards 
  • Present ourselves fit for work
  • Provide and wear the right PPE
  • Take 20 seconds to ensure that a job can be done safely
  • Identify hazards and take action
  • Work safely and behave responsibly
  • Challenge others over HSW issues and prepare to be challenged
  • Report incidents, openly and honestly, and learn from them


Liv Garfield (2016)
