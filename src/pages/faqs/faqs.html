Help

Health

 Ensuring that employees are ‘Fit and capable for work’: safeguarding against harm to employees as a result of exposure to work place hazards (i.e. chemicals, noise, asbestos, excessive work pressures)

Safety

 Ensuring you are not injured by an accident as a result of your work.

Wellbeing

 Proactively engaging employees to participate in mental and physical health campaigns to promote a positive, present and productive workforce.

Security

 To record observations of security good practice, instances of non-compliance and security incidents.

Water Quality

 To record observations of potential hazards that could impact on the quality of our treated water as well as record examples of where good practice or proactive intervention has removed a hazard.

Environmental Hazards

 To record observations where potential hazards exist that may impact on the environment and where good practice and proactive intervention has removed a hazard.
