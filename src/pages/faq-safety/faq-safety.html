FAQS

Incident

 An event which has caused injury or illness, or has caused some damage to items, property or vehicles and had the potential to cause injury or illness.

Hazard

 Nothing has ‘happened’ but you are aware of a situation that has the potential to cause an incident.

Positive Behaviour

 Nothing has ‘happened’ but you are aware of or have seen an example of a positive behaviour or a great working practice.

Unsafe Behaviour

 Nothing has ‘happened’ but you are aware of or have seen an example of an unsafe behaviour that has the potential to cause an incident.
